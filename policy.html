Privacy Policy

We at Astroway ("we" or "astroway") take the privacy of the information you provide us very seriously. We created this privacy policy to demonstrate our commitment to privacy. Please read this carefully to understand how we use any information you provide us at astroway.com.

  • Astroway guarantees confidentiality of our members' identity, birth details and any forecasts made using those details as much as possible under the applicable legal, regulatory and security environment.
  • Astroway further guarantees that no direct or indirect use will be made by Astroway of any information that is revealed in the prediction for a member, except for the explicit purpose of communicating the horoscope charts and predictions back to the member.
  • We allow members to access their user profiles and change information, as they deem necessary.
  • We do not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to anyone.

As we keep adding new services and items, we suggest that you check back from time to time in order to understand how we treat your information. In case of change in our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what personal information we collect, how we use it and under what circumstances we disclose it. Your continued use of the site will constitute your acknowledgment of our Privacy Policy.

Index

The items below provide an index to the information that follows:

  1. What personal information we collect?

    1. Registration Data

      During membership registration, Astroway collects personal information including your name, gender, birth information, email address, phone number and your place of birth and residence. This information is used to calculate your accurate horoscope. Your e-mail address or phone number together with a password is used to secure your profile and to make our personalized communication services available to you. The information is collected at astroway.com or any of its subdomains like vartaapi.astroway.com. You do not have to provide any personal information to use Astroway services. However, if you choose not to become a member of Astroway, we may not be able to provide you with some of the other services that require such information (for example, personalized free horoscopes).

    2. Image

      Our app utilizes images exclusively for display purposes within the application, particularly as profile pictures. We do not upload these images to our servers, nor do we employ them for any other purposes beyond their intended use within the app. Providing an image as a profile picture is optional.

    3. Log Files, IP Address and Cookies

      When you visit astroway.com and interact with the services and tools that reside there, Astroway and third parties with whom Astroway has contracted to provide services may collect information on your actions. The purpose of this information collection is generally to gather broad demographic information that is not personal. We also use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our software or server and to administer our website. We collect browser types used, screen resolutions, and time spent on the website to analyze trends, administer and troubleshoot the site, and to enhance the performance and services of the site.

    4. Through Order Forms

      We gather information required to fulfill our paid services through our order forms. This information is maintained in strict confidence. As stated earlier, we guarantee confidentiality of our members' identity, birth details and the prediction made, subject to applicable legal, regulatory and security requirements. Astroway further guarantees that no direct or indirect use will be made by Astroway of any information that is revealed in the prediction for a member, except for the explicit purpose of communicating the horoscope charts and predictions back to the member.

    5. Other information

      Any other information we receive via email or other method of contact regarding specific services or products, as a general rule, is not made part of your member profile and is used only to address the specific need or concern you communicated to us.

  2. Security

    We DO NOT collect any payment information on our website. Payment information is collected and processed in a secure manner by third-party payment gateways. We follow reasonable industry standards, technical measures and procedural safeguards to protect against unauthorized access and use of your supplied information. However, no web site can fully eliminate all risks.

  3. How Users may request data deletion?

    • All personal information related to the user account will be deleted on deletion of such account.
    • The user can request to delete their account by sending an email to customercare@astroway.com.

  4. Other web site links

    Our web site may contain links to other web sites. Astroway is not responsible for the privacy practices followed by those web sites.

  5. Contacting us

    If you have any questions about this privacy statement, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this web site, you can contact us at customercare@astroway.com.
